Rules And Regulations


The rules for group eligibility and submissions can be navigated below:


4 to 8 Music Groups will be selected as finalists. Finalists will be given the opportunity to perform in Bailey Hall on the evening of March 12th, 2022.

Winner(s) of the Bailey Hall concert competition will be notified by the end of Cornell's Spring Break, and earn an opportunity to perform at Slope Day events.

Neither Jazz+ nor any of the event's organizers or sponsors own any of the content submitted by any Music Group.

Good luck!
